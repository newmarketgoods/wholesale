Coverlets
& Duvets

Our coverlets are weighty enough to keep you warm, but way crisper than your winter woolens. Use as a lightweight cover in warmer months or as a vibrant layering piece in cooler ones.

Duvets feature the same cotton textiles as our coverlets, with matching top and bottom, and hidden button closures. Duvets do not have tassel or selvedge detailing.

Designed in San Francisco & Brooklyn. Handwoven from 100% cotton in Bangladesh.
