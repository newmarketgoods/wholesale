Process

Please review our policies. Purchase order requests can be placed using the linked form below, or directly on this page (please provide us with your resale license information).

Submit forms to info@newmarketgoods.com and we'll follow-up to confirm details. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact us!

Policies

Order Minimum See specific products for minimum order quantities.
Payment Method We can accept payments via credit card or PayPal.
Shipping We can provide quotes for shipping based on the quantity and contents of your order request, as well as the ship-to location. We generally ship using FedEx or USPS.
Returns We accept returns for defective or damaged items within 15 days of receipt of goods.
Cancellation Orders cannot be cancelled once production has been initiated.
